Learn More About Our Initiative & Goals

Mission Essentials is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of those in need. Our goal is to provide necessities such as hygiene products, clothing, school supplies, and food to individuals and communities struggling to make ends meet. Our team of dedicated volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that every person who comes to us receives the help they need.
